Platform Overview

One Rifle. Every Terrain.

The Tikka T3x Ace Game bridges the gap between the dedicated hunting field rifle and the precision competition platform. Sharing the same modular Ace chassis as the T3x Ace Target, the Game variant distinguishes itself with a fluted medium-contour barrel — lighter for mountain carries, accurate enough for long-range ethical shots.

Where the Ace Target prioritizes raw bench performance with its heavier barrel, the Ace Game is the all-rounder — equally at home on a NRL Hunter stage as it is on a dawn stalk. The modular chassis accepts all T3, T3x, and T1x barreled actions, meaning a single stock investment covers your entire Tikka ecosystem.

Calibers span .223 Rem through .300 Win Mag, with cold hammer-forged barrels engineered in Riihimäki, Finland to the same exacting tolerances Sako uses in their flagship line. Every Tikka ships with a sub-MOA guarantee.
